Marian Mills

Marian Mills is a still life painter. She depicts everyday environments in intimate, bright colours. She creates paintings that express atmosphere and emotion and bring life to objects that are often overlooked. Her abstract art style prioritises feeling over detail giving her art a unique movement. She is inspired by Matisse, Bonnard, and Vermeer. She is available to commission
